About Our System

A revolutionary platform designed to enhance the final year project experience for UiTM students and supervisors

Empowering Academic Excellence

The UiTM FYP Management System is a state-of-the-art platform designed to streamline the entire final year project lifecycle. Our system connects students, supervisors, and administrators in a cohesive ecosystem that simplifies project management, tracking, and evaluation.

With intuitive tools and a user-friendly interface, students can focus on what truly matters - creating impactful research and innovative projects that showcase their academic capabilities.

Supervisors gain powerful tools to monitor progress, provide timely feedback, and ensure that every student receives the guidance they need to succeed in their academic journey.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that shape the UiTM FYP experience

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive, user-friendly platform that empowers UiTM students to successfully complete high-quality final year projects. We aim to foster academic excellence, innovation, and research skills through streamlined project management and effective supervision.

Our Vision

To become the leading final year project management system in Malaysian higher education, setting the standard for academic project excellence. We envision a future where every student has the tools and support needed to produce impactful, industry-relevant research.
